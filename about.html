01 - Who I Am

Got into it through gaming.
Stayed for everything else.

I'm Johnny El-Ammar, an IT graduate from Enfield, London. Like most people my age I grew up gaming, but at some point the interest shifted from playing to understanding - how does this actually work, what's running underneath it, can I build something myself.

That curiosity is what led me to study IT, build my own home lab, find my passion for cybersecurity, and tinker with web development (mainly for this website). I'm driven and I don't really do half measures - if I'm working on something I want to do it properly. All in, Or nothing.
